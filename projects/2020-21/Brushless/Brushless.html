Brushless

Paint over generated artworks through the use of a particle system.

Description

Brushless aims at realizing virtual paintings by generating an artwork and then paint over it through the use of a particle system.

After the generation, the image is then painted just by moving a hand in front of a sensor! The hand movement drives the particles in order to give to the user the feeling of a real brush stroke.


Challenges, accomplishment and lessons learned


Technology
